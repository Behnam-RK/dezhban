dezhban is a network kill switch: it cuts your internet the moment your VPN drops
or your exit lands in a country you have blocked.

This installer sets up everything, in one step:

  • the dezhban command-line tool        (/usr/local/bin/dezhban)
  • the Dezhban menubar app              (/Applications/Dezhban.app)
  • the background service                (registered with launchd)

You will be asked for your password ONCE, now. After that, the everyday
operations — blocking, unblocking, and switching VPNs — never ask again: the
background service performs them for you.

Nothing is blocked yet. The installer registers the service but deliberately
leaves it stopped, because a kill switch configured by guesswork is how you get
locked out of your own machine. You choose the settings, then you start it.

The next screen tells you how.
